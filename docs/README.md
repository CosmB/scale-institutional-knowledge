# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

This directory contains OctoAcme's project management playbook for running cross-functional work from idea to release. The process starts with a lightweight initiation phase to align on business outcomes, stakeholders, success metrics, and go/no-go criteria. Once approved, planning turns goals into a prioritized backlog, defined milestones, a clear Definition of Done, and early risk and dependency tracking.

Execution is driven by regular team rhythms (standups, delivery syncs, and demos), small pull requests, and quality gates such as testing, CI checks, and review policies. The process emphasizes visibility through project boards, delivery metrics, and documented escalation paths so blockers are surfaced and resolved quickly.

Risk management and communication run continuously through the lifecycle via a maintained risk register, stakeholder updates, and incident communications when needed. Releases follow a structured checklist with pre-release validation, deployment verification, and rollback readiness. Retrospectives after milestones and releases close the loop by converting lessons learned into owned improvement actions.
